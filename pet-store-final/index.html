Welcome To Paws & Claws Pet Store

At Paws & Claws Pet Store, we believe every pet deserves a loving home and exceptional care. Our local, family operated store in Boston offers healthy pets, trusted supplies, professional grooming services, and a welcoming environment for animal lovers of all ages.

We proudly serve families throughout the Boston area and strive to provide excellent customer service every day.
